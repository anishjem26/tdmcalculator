🔄 NOTA KONVERSI UNIT PENTING
Aminoglycoside:
mg/L = mcg/mL (setara, tiada konversi diperlukan)
Laporan lab biasanya dalam mg/L atau mcg/mL
Sesetengah lab laporkan dalam µmol/L:
µmol/L × 0.4772 = mg/L (Gentamicin)
µmol/L × 0.5853 = mg/L (Amikacin)
Serum Creatinine:
µmol/L ÷ 88.4 = mg/dL
mg/dL × 88.4 = µmol/L
Faktor konversi: 88.4
(MW Creatinine = 113.12 g/mol × 0.001 × 1000/1.13 ≈ 88.4)
